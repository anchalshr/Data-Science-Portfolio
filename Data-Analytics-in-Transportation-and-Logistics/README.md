## Project Title: Business Data Management Capstone Project 
Strategies to acquire new commercial partnerships for furthering the growth of a truck transport company without significantly burdening its finances

## Objective of the Project
1. To make the current system more effective at handling client demands.
2. To identify the key tactics that support the development of business relationships without putting much financial burden on the company.
3. To find solutions for problems like fake loading and transparency problems encountered in routine business order requests.

 ## Dataset 
 The data used in the analysis process is taken from the order books that are maintained on regular basis by the company from April 2022 to November 2022.
 ODC (payload capacity: 10 tons to 100 tons), Normal truck service(9 tons to 15 tons), mini truck and packers & movers service (4 tons to 8 tons)
